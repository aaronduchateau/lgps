Who we are ?

We’re a Saint Louis-Based Legal Tech startup focused on eliminating avoidable legal mistakes. We talked to all our users and created our product based on their feedback. Legal GPS helps you anticipate your legal problems, understand the consequences, and act to solve them.

the Legal GPS mission

Our mission is to eliminate all avoidable legal mistakes made by businesses. Our content and product are focused on helping you solve problems you “don’t know you don’t know” so that you can focus on your business. Our goal is to help you succeed.
